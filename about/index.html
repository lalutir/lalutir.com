Background

I'm Lars Timmer, a Data Science student at The Hague University of Applied Sciences, studying Applied Data Science and Artificial Intelligence. I've recently worked as a teaching assistant for first-year students, helping them through their first year of study.

I like building data pipelines and using that data to train models — my passion for football is what led me to build a tournament simulator for the knockout stages of the 2026 World Cup. Outside of coursework, I'm a field hockey umpire for the Royal Dutch Hockey Association (KNHB) and previously coached hockey at my local club, MHCZ. Until July 2026 I was treasurer of the Live Video Committee at Proteus-Eretes, managing the finances for the committee that organizes livestream coverage of rowing races across the Netherlands.
